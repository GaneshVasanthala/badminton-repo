📖 Format

How it works

From pool stage to the final — the complete tournament format

1

Pool Stage

24 players split into 4 pools of 6. Everyone plays each other in their pool.

2

Qualification

Top 2 from each pool advance. 8 players qualify for the knockout stage.

3

Quarter-finals

8 players → 4 winners. Best of 3 sets, 21 points per set.

4

Semi-finals

4 players → 2 finalists. High intensity best of 3 matches.

5

Grand Final

The last 2 standing battle it out for the Shuttle Showdown trophy!
